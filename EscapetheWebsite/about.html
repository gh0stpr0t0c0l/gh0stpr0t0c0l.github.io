About Us

What is Escape the Website?

It’s an escape room, but different. Instead of locked doors, you’ll find hidden layers. Instead of keys, you’ll use logic, perception, and curiosity.

Some puzzles are straightforward. Others require looking at things from a different angle. A few might feel like they’re looking at you.

Progress won’t always be obvious, but trust the process—most users make it through eventually.

Just remember: escaping is only part of the challenge.
