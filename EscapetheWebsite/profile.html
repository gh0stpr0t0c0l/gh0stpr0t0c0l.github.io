Rachel M. Porter

Role: User Experience Liaison

About You: “A seamless experience is a forgettable one. The best puzzles are the ones you don’t realize you’re solving.” I make sure Escape the Website works exactly the way it should—which is to say, in ways you don’t always notice. A good user experience isn’t just about navigation or interface design; it’s about guiding decisions, shaping perception, and ensuring every interaction feels inevitable.

My work focuses on:

  • Optimizing pathways so users never feel trapped—until they are.
  • Reducing friction between the user and their next choice (even when they don’t realize they’re making one).
  • Designing feedback loops that encourage the right kind of progress.

I've also been deeply involved in the development of essential programs such as Remnant, Pathfinder Pro, EchoLogger, ClairifAI, and ComplianceCheck.

I spend a lot of time thinking about patterns, perception, and the ways people find meaning in what they see—or don’t see. If you ever feel like the site is leading you somewhere, that’s not a coincidence.

And yes, my office door is open. (Not physically, of course. That would be… inconvenient.)
